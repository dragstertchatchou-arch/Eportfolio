Smart Home Model (IoT)

Arduino Bluetooth App Dev C++

Description: Design and realization of a remote-controlled home automation model. Integration of sensors (temperature, gas, motion) and actuators (lighting, alarm) controlled by an Arduino board. Development of a dedicated mobile application for control via Bluetooth module.

Skills Acquired: This project strengthened my teamwork skills through technical task coordination. I developed skills in embedded programming, electronic circuit design, and system integration (Hardware/Software).

DC Motor Design

Electromagnetism Mechanics DIY

Description: Design and assembly of a series-excited direct current motor, made from recycled materials (copper wire for winding, permanent magnets, handmade collector). The goal was to build a functional machine from scratch.

Skills Acquired: This practical realization materialized theoretical concepts of magnetism. I deepened my understanding of the internal workings of rotating machines, particularly the crucial role of the brush-collector system and electromagnetic torque creation.
